Travel Planning

Automated flight-fare tracking for upcoming Seahawks-itinerary and family trips out of AVL. Cloudflare Worker + D1 captures fares daily at 7 AM ET via SerpAPI; Next.js dashboard renders live data; redundant Perplexity tasks deliver alerts.
